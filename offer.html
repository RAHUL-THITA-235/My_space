Delhi (/'d?li/, Hindustani pronunciation: [d??lli?] Dilli), officially the National Capital Territory of Delhi, is the Capital territory of India.[4] It has a population of about 11 million and a metropolitan population of about 16.3 million, making it the second most populous city and second most populous urban agglomeration in India.[5][6] Such is the nature of urban expansion in Delhi that its growth has expanded beyond the NCT to incorporate towns in neighbouring states and at its largest extent can count a population of about 25 million residents as of 2014.[7]
