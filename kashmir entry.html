Tourism in Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir(Travel Information):


By Plane


Flights operate to Jammu, Srinagar and Leh. We'll take tourists on either airline company:
[1]Air India
[2]Jet Konnected
[3]GoAir India
[4]Indigo Airlines
[5]Spice Jet



By Train


The last stop on the railways line north is Udhampur, where we will catch onward buses or hire SUVs/MUVs(Tata Sumo / ToyataToyota Innova/ Mahindra Scorpio). However it is better to get down in Jammu and catch a taxi from there as these are more readily available.



By Bus


There are two ways to get in by land- via Jammu and up to Srinagar or via Manali in Himachal Pradesh and up to Leh.



Transportation Within

  • Buses

    •  are are operated by J&K SRTC to most points around the state. They offer package tour to Gulmarg, Shonmarg, Yusmarg, Ular lake, City tour, etc. There is Tourist information center, where one get the tickets plus info.

  • 4 wheel drive jeeps

    •  are quicker, a little more expensive and reach more locations. Private hire jeeps are also available.
