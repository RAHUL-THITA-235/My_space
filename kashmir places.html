-Capital of Jammu and Kashmir, a popular hill station. Most popular tourist place in Srinagar is Dal Lake, with houseboats called shikharas.

Gulmarg

-Gulmarg is well known for its amazing natural beauty and it is counted as one of the unique tourist destinations in the world. Gulmarg is surrounded by dense forest. The natural beauty accompanied with clean fresh air and a beauiful environment highlights Gulmarg as a major tourist destination.

Sonmarg

-The way to reach Sonmarg or the Meadow of the gold is form the Sindh Valley. This valley also shows more amazing facet in Kashmir. Sonmarg is located at an altitude of around 2730 meters from the sea level; it has snow-coveredmountains as its backdrop against the sky.
